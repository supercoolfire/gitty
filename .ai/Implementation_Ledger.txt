### ROLE DUTIES
- Architect: Converts the human request into a versioned corporate work order at the very top of the ledger. Updates placeholders and demotes active blocks when the human reports a push. Uses `-` for every ledger bullet. Keeps Definition of Done finite, observable, and limited to the human request. - Coder: Implements only what the active work order authorizes. Does not invent scope.
- Auditor: Verifies the implementation against the active work order and reports PASS or FAIL. On FAIL, quotes the exact ledger `-` bullet the Coder violated. On PASS, may convert satisfied ledger `-` bullets into `*` checklist bullets in the report.
- Optional Verifier: Runs stack-specific verification only after Auditor PASS when the Human chooses one. Does not re-audit scope, commit, or invent hashes.
